About Us

Do you have an issue with inviting all your friends and family to your potluck? Ever have more than one kind of dish? Do your guests ever hound you about the date and then forget to show up? Then aren't you in luck! We are a small team of web developers that wish to solve all your potluck planning issues. Creat your event and add a list of dishes that your guests can bring. When your guests come to view your event, they'll see what dishes have been claimed and which ones are still available and even RSVP. You'll be able to see how many guests you'll have and adjust the amount of dishes accordingly. So the next time you're planning a potluck, just remember: we take the hassle out of planning a potluck!
